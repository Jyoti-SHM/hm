Success Story of Hotel Supreme Comforts

We take pride in sharing the success stories for one of our clients and the strong relationships we’ve built with them. Our commitment to nurturing these bonds continues to drive our mutual growth and success.

Hotel Supreme Comforts, a brand-new property established in 2024, is ideally situated near Andheri Airport and close to Chakala metro station. We began collaborating with this hotel in May 24, initially focusing on a one-time setup process. Our services included listing the property on OTA’s, establishing promotions, and ensuring its presence on major platforms like Google and TripAdvisor. Once the setup was complete, we provided OTA handling training, ensuring the hotel was fully equipped for independent management.

After experiencing our efficient setup process and recognizing our understanding of their needs, Hotel Supreme Comforts felt confident in building a mutually beneficial relationship with us. As a leading revenue management and digital marketing company, we offer a comprehensive range of services under one roof. This holistic approach reassured the hotel that with the same level of focus and dedication to managing their online brand presence, they achieved significant success and secured excellent results.

Starting on June 24, our expert revenue management team began working closely with Hotel Supreme Comfort. We conducted a thorough analysis of the hotel's scope and operations, gaining valuable market insights as we leverage strong relationships with OTAs. This collaborative effort was crucial for success, and the hotel demonstrated a strong commitment to cooperation. They actively sought genuine reviews from guests, went the extra mile to ensure guest comfort, and helped with airport transport services, making guests feel valued for their money. Not to mention the new property competes with the well-established hotel brands in the area, such as Hotel Aurika, Suba International, Bloom Hub, Byaris International, and Oriental Aster, therefore we need to strategically position ourselves to differentiate and attract guests. We established a strong foundation for ranking and ratings by focusing on key performance metrics. The hotel maintained a steadfast commitment to achieving high ratings, which contributed to its visibility and success. As a result, the hotel consistently ranked among the top 10 hotels in the Chakala area and also secured a good position on Tripadvisor looking at the competition and its new presence to the online world.

As a result of our focused efforts on June 24, the hotel achieved a revenue exceeding 15 Lakhs, with an ARR of approx 3000++ which was a gratifying outcome for both parties. The momentum continued into July 24, with the hotel’s revenue surpassing 20 Lakhs with an ARR of approx 3200++ and the story is continuing balancing healthy financial benchmarks.

This success story underscores several key factors:

  • Effective Set-Up: Ensuring a meticulous listing on all OTA and Meta sites is essential.
  • In-Depth Market Analysis: Expertise in market knowledge is vital.
  • Hotel Cooperation: A hotel’s dedication to collaborating and striving for excellence is crucial.
  • Guest Centric : Focus on the needs, preferences, and experiences of guests, This approach prioritizes creating positive and personalized experiences to them.
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Continuous attention and adjustments help maintain financial success

To sum it up, a business thrives by being guest-centric, offering value for money, and valuing guest feedback. Success comes when both business partners share a common goal, listen to each other, and adapt to changes and challenges. Staying relevant in the market requires following trends and embracing change, and Hotel Supreme Comforts exemplifies these principles. We deeply appreciate our partnership with Supreme Comfort and look forward to continued success together.

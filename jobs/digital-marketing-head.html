Job Description

We’re seeking a dynamic Digital Marketer Head to lead and execute data-driven marketing strategies across digital channels, including SEO, SEM, social media, and more. In this role, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to enhance user experience, drive customer acquisition, and increase retention.

Key Responsibilities

  • Campaign Strategy & Management: Develop, execute, and optimize multi-channel marketing campaigns (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, SEO, email, etc.) to drive business growth.
  • Team Leadership: Lead and mentor the digital marketing team, ensuring collaboration and alignment with overall business goals.
  • Content Strategy: Oversee content creation and social media strategy, ensuring consistent brand messaging and engagement across platforms.
  • Performance Tracking: Use analytics tools to monitor campaign performance, generate insights, and implement strategies for continuous improvement.
  • Trend Adaptation: Stay informed on the latest digital marketing trends and best practices, guiding the team in incorporating them into strategies
  • Budget Management: Manage and allocate the marketing budget effectively to maximize ROI across channels.
  • Client Collaboration: Build and maintain relationships with clients, ensuring alignment on campaign goals and outcomes.
  • Platform Proficiency: Leverage tools and platforms (Google Analytics, Meta Ads Manager, etc.) to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.

This role requires strong leadership skills, strategic thinking, and expertise across various digital marketing channels.

Requirements:

  • Strong knowledge of Google Ads and Meta Ads.
  • Proven experience in developing and executing growth marketing strategies.
  • Ability to analyze data and make informed decisions to drive campaign efficiency.
  • Excellent collaboration skills and the ability to lead cross-functional teams.

If you’re passionate about digital marketing and driving business growth through innovative strategies, apply now!

Benefits & Opportunities:

  • Enhancement of Management skills.
  • Enhancement of Soft skills & Interpersonal skills
  • Team Building skills.
  • Network with Industry stalwarts and Hotel owners
  • Incentives
  • Travel Benefits
  • Trainings
